External Analytics Consultant

As an external analytics consultant I curated diverse datasets including traffic patterns, property values, business engagement, and calls for service, supplementing analysis with a Qualtrics survey on business satisfaction to enhance data insights for Iowa City's South of 6 Business District. With my team, I transformed raw data into practical Excel and Tableau dashboards effectively communicating complex information to stakeholders, aiding in data-driven decision-making for future district progress. To conclude we presented findings and dashboards to the South of 6 Board, demonstrating a deep understanding of data analysis, visualization techniques, and persuasive communication.

Krause Fund Equity Analyst

As an Equity research analyst for the Krause Fund, a $675K portfolio managed by undergraduate students enrolled in the Applied Equity Valuation course, I performed industry-wide and individual company research on the technology sector and of the current portfolio holding, ASML, to build financial models using economic profit, discounted cash flow, dividend discount, and relative valuation analyses. This included a comprehensive Buy/Sell recommendation presentation to the Fund’s advisory board for final portfolio decision on the recommendation.
